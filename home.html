Meet the Party

His

Tony Giesbrecht - Best Man

      Tony is James's closest brother both in terms of age and relationship. Tony is a 27 year old husband to Cassandra, and father to 3 beautiful children; the ring bearer Braxton, Jase, and Remington, born just weeks before the big day! Tony currently drives truck regionally and is loving it! Despite wildy different interests Tony and James have maintained a special bond that only brothers can have. James was a groomsman at Tonys wedding back in 2011 and it was only appropiate that Tony would be the best man at his best friends wedding.

Jacob Giesbrecht - Groomsman

      Jake is the oldest of the Giesbrecht children, a full 15 years older than James. He married Ruth 17 years ago and has since become the father to 7 children, including the flower girl Lorraina. Jake keeps busy with his Steinbach based business, Jacob's Locksmithing. James was still quite young when Jake moved out to study at Steinbach Bible College but he remembers living at home with his big bro fondly. Jake is a big man with an even bigger heart.

Willy Friesen - Groomsman

      Willy was James's first brother-in-law when he married James's sister Chrisie many years ago. Willy is a father to 3 kids, the other flower girl Sydney, Felix and Lincoln. Willy is always tons of fun and probably taught James a few things he shouldn't of when he was young. Now that Marissa is joining the family, Willy will finally have someone to talk about hockey with.

Hers

Brooke Gauthier - Maid of Honor

      Brooke is the baby of 4 girls, when Marissa would visit she always felt like she was one of the sisters! Brooke and Marissa were able to take a trip to Hawaii during spring break of grade 12, and were lucky enough to have their senior portraits done on the beaches in Hawaii! They made many memories that will stick with them forever! Brooke is a flight attendant with Sunwing airlines and Marissa hopes to be able to be on one of her future flights to see her in action!

Kallie Dyck - Bridesmaid

      Kallie and Marissa have been best friends since before they were born. Their moms were friends so it only made sense that they were too! Marissa and Kallie along with the maid of honour, Brooke, were inseparable in high school, doing everything together, and even got to take a trip to Mexico together! Kallie was the first of the group to have a baby, and is the most incredible mom to her adorable baby Theo! Kallie is also getting married this summer to her fiance Dylan, the girls been having tons of fun planning together and look forward to continuing on in the same adventures of life!

Kelly Lemay - Bridesmaid

      Kelly is the sister Marissa always wanted but never had! Kel’s felt part of the family for the past 5 years as she’s been dating Marissa’s brother since grade 11! She’s been a hair stylist and makeup artist since high school and you’ll see some of her amazing work at the wedding! Besides sharing clothes, Marissa and Kel share the same interests in trendy coffee shops and try to get out together as much as they can!

Theirs

Sydney Friesen - Flower Girl

      Sydney is James's niece and oldest daughter of groomsman Willy. She is currently in grade 3 and loves school and her gymnastics classes. Sydney and the other flower girl Lorraina are best friends and are super excited to be a part of the wedding!

Lorraina Giesbrecht - Flower Girl

Braxton Giesbrecht - Ring Bearer
